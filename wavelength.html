HOW TO PLAY

  • There are 5 images at the top of the screen, each one has been assigned a value 1-20

  • 1 being one of the best things in all history of things, 20 being one of the worst.

  • Enter a guess for each image on where you think that thing falls on the that scale

  • If you are within 2 numbers of that object the square will highlight yellow, if it is correct the square will be green

  • The number to the right of the square shows you what the average wavelength for all items is

  • How many guesses will it take you to guess each item right?
